The Monty Hall Problem

The Monty Hall problem is a famous probability puzzle:

  1. There are three doors, behind one is a car, behind the others are goats.
  2. You pick a door, hoping to win the car.
  3. The host, who knows what's behind each door, opens one of the other doors to reveal a goat.
  4. The host then asks if you want to stick with your original choice or switch to the remaining door.
  5. What should you do? Is it better to switch or stay?
